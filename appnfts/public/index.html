Beginning

Angry Birds, Flappy Bird, Duck Hunt rocked your childhood ? It's time to discover new ones in the blockchain Ethereum ! You will be able to play with these NFTs birds that YOU own in a Web 3.0 world.
The eggs will hatch at the end of the countdown and the birds you owned will be revealed according to the rarity.

It all started in the year 1984.

A game named Duck Hunt was first released on April 21, 1984 in Japan on Famicom and in North America on the arcade system with a plastic gun. The object of the game was to shoot ducks in order to improve his score and increase his level. A revolution.

From this date with this mythical game, players will play many bird games such as Angry Birds or Flappy Birds.

But now is the time to finally play the bird games that you own only!

This adventure is just the beginning of a huge story that begins with the sale of bird NFTs to play in a 3.0 world. The new revolution is coming...
